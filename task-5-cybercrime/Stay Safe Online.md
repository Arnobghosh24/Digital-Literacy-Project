# 🔒 Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone online  
- Verify links and websites before clicking, especially in emails or messages  
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logins  
- Be cautious of unknown calls or messages creating urgency or fear  

## 💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required for sending money  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept collect requests from unknown people  
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before confirming any payment  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents immediately at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** for urgent financial fraud cases  

## 📌 Reminder
Stay alert, think before you act, and always verify before you trust.
